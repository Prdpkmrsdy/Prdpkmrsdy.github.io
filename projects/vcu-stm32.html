Automotive Vehicle Control Unit (VCU) for SAE Team

Designed and developed an automotive VCU based on an STM32L496RET6 microcontroller with CAN, GPS-GSM, IMU, secure authentication IC, ADC/DAC signal conditioning, and opto-isolated digital I/O for vehicle mode control, safety monitoring, and diagnostics.

STM32L496 CAN GPS-GSM IMU Opto-isolated I/O HAL
Firmware
  • Interrupt-driven STM32 HAL firmware: CAN, UART, ADC, GPIO, timers
  • Fault handling + diagnostics-ready structure
  • UART logging and bring-up support
Power & Hardware
  • Buck/LDO regulation and battery charging integration
  • Voltage/current/temperature sensing
  • MCU-controlled power sequencing for robust operation

Automotive Vehicle Control Unit (VCU) for SAE Team

Designed and developed an automotive VCU based on an STM32L496RET6 microcontroller with CAN, GPS-GSM, IMU, secure authentication IC, ADC/DAC signal conditioning, and opto-isolated digital I/O for vehicle mode control, safety monitoring, and diagnostics.

Flyback DC-DC Converter (800V → 12V, 72W) – Planar Transformer

Designed an automotive-grade isolated flyback DC-DC converter using a planar transformer reference design and INN94908C-Q1 controller. The work includes HV EMI filtering, RCD snubber optimization, and TL431/optocoupler-based feedback loop design for stable regulation and compact packaging.

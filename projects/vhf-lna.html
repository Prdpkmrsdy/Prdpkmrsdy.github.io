VHF-band Low Noise Amplifier (125–160 MHz)

Designed a PGA-103+ based LNA PCB for 125–160 MHz with high gain and low noise figure, 50Ω matching, flexible power/bias options, band-pass filtering, ESD protection, and RF-grounded layout optimization.
PGA-103+125–160 MHzAltium SystemVueBPF (LC)ESD
RF Performance Targets
  • ~18 dB gain, ~0.5 dB NF (design target)
  • High linearity (OIP3 baseline ~35 dBm; extendable)
  • 50Ω matching and low insertion loss layout
Front-End Architecture
  • High-Q LC band-pass filter (125–160 MHz)
  • AC coupling, low-ripple power conditioning
  • TLV1117-based regulated bias network
